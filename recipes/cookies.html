Cooky Cookies

Description

Cookies, the kind you eat.

You will like them.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flavor

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar with the flavor in a large mixing bowl. Electric is allowed.
  3. Stir the flour with the previous mix.
  4. Use a scoop to get out cookie sized dough blobs, and plop em on the baking sheet.
  5. Bake until done.
  6. Let cool, you don't want to burn your tongue!
  7. Eat it!
