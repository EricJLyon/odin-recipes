Lasagna Garfieldaise

Description

A delicious traditional Italian lasagna that is sure to please cats*!

*please do not feed lasagna to cats

Ingredients

  • Pasta Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In an appropriate dish, layer noodles, meat, cheese, and sauce several times.
  3. Bake until it is cooked through.
  4. Let cool, you don't want to burn your tongue!
  5. Eat it!
