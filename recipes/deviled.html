Deviled Eggs

Description

Unholy eggs delicious enough for the devil himself.

Only served in hell.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs, hard-boiled
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Vinegar
  • Smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Halve eggs longwise.
  2. Mix yolks, mayo, mustard, vinegar.
  3. Plop mix into egg white halves.
  4. Sprinkle with smoked paprika.
  5. Eat it!
